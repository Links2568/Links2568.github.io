## About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the **University of Michigan, Ann Arbor**, majoring in **Honors Data Science & Statistics** with a minor in **History**. Previously, I studied Statistics & Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My research interests span generative AI, privacy-aware machine learning, and human-computer interaction.

## Research Interests

- **Generative AI:** music and audio generation, human-GenAI interaction
- **Privacy & Security:** geolocation attack/defense with diffusion models and VLMs
- **Statistical Machine Learning:** dimension reduction, Bayesian methods, wearable data analysis
- **Climate Science:** aerosol-cloud interactions, statistical modeling

  <br>Building a universal optical music recognition framework for visually complex notations; exploring real-time human–GenAI interaction for controllable music generation.
- **Ambient Intelligence Lab**, UMich — Research Assistant, Oct 2025 – Present
  <br>Developing privacy-driven geolocation attack and defense frameworks using diffusion models and VLMs; designing intelligent IoT sensing systems with Moiré-pattern optical sensors.
- **Energy Distance Dimension Reduction**, UMich — URPS, Jan 2025 – Present
  <br>Applying sufficient dimension reduction to multilevel wearable data from bone marrow transplant patients; re-implementing SDR algorithms in JAX with 7,000× speedup.
- **Lab of Atmospheric and Earth System Sciences**, Nanjing University — Summer Research Intern, May – Aug 2024
  <br>Applied statistical machine learning to study aerosol-cloud interactions and improved simulations of aerosol optical properties and climate effects.
- **E2-E Lab**, UIUC — Undergraduate Research Assistant, Aug 2023 – May 2024
  <br>Conducted airflow experiments with PIV data processing and CFD simulations to analyze particle–airflow interactions in complex 3D geometries.
